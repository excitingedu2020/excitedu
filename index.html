Solution to making distance learning more entertaining

There are many ways to make distance learning more joyful and fun for students. Instead of feeling gloomy during different times, a short game of bingo or kahoot could make class more lively.

  • Quizlet Live, quizizz, kahoot, gimkit
  • Online Bingo, Online Pictionary, Online Sudoku
  • Short games at beginning of class(2 truths 1 lie, 20 questions)
  • Listen to music to rest our eyes after every lesson

There are also other things which can be done to help students feel a little more energized during class, such as:

  • Students take break to stretch and look away from computer every 20-25 minutes
  • Since students are all at home, they can get a healthy snack to have during classes.
  • Talk to your family during lunch and try to be away from a laptop for some time
